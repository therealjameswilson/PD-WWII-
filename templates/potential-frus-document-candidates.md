# 50 Potential FRUS Documents Not Found in Existing FRUS Exact Screens

This register lists 50 candidate documents or file-unit targets for a retro FRUS volume on World War II public diplomacy. Each row has a NARA/FDR locator, a History.state.gov exact-title or file-number search URL, and cross-references into the packet, duplicate, citation, and readiness registers.

Exact public History.state.gov screens were checked on 2026-06-02; each returned no public exact result for the attached title, phrase, or file-number query. Treat this as a candidate screen, not a final FRUS duplicate ruling: promoted documents still need source copies, broad topical searches, result counts, and final editor review.

## Use This First

1. Open the candidate row and follow the catalog or FDR/NARA link.
2. Save a source copy or reproduction request under the related pull sheet.
3. Attach exact and broad History.state.gov search proof in the duplicate-gate register.
4. Update the linked citation, readiness, receipt-QA, selection, and annotation rows after source copies arrive.
